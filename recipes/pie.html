Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup apple juice
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 6 golden delicous apples - peeled with core removed and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • Crust and top for a 9 inch double crust pie

Directions

  1. In a saucepan, combine apple juice, 1/2 cup sugar, and butter over medium heat stirring occasionally until the sugar has melted then add apples and cook until the slices are tender ~10 min.
  2. Mix the rest of the sugar and cornstarch and then combine with the fruit mixture and bring to a boil until the sauce thickens ~2 min.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F/175 degrees C and place a baking sheet on an oven rack.
  4. Roll out the bottom crust that should line a 9 inch pie pan and put the pie filling into it, cover with the top crust and seal it, cut/poke holes in the top to allow steam to vent.
  5. Bake on the baking sheet until crust is golden brown ~45 min.
