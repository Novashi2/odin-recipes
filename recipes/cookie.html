Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 additional tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 additional teaspoons ground cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F/ 200 C and line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Combine the sugar, butter, and eggs in a bowl until creamy and then add the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt in a seperate bowl and stir both together until the dough holds together.
  3. Mix the white sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl until they are well combined.
  4. Form the dough into balls that have about 2 teaspoon in each and then roll in the cinnamon-sugar coating using the additional ingredients andn then place the balls about 2 inches apart onto your parchment lined baking sheet.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven on the center rack for 7 minutes and allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes until you transfer them to a wire rack to cool.
